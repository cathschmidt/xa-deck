The Thesis

1

Economies compute value.

  • Ruled by numbers: Economic processes are fundamentally computational.
  • Networks compute: They coordinate according to a formal logic; a network language that defines what value is and how it is calculated, economic protocols.
  • Distributing power: These protocols dictate how value is recorded, calculated, understood and utilized. The network computations they enable determine the outcomes, limitations, and agency given to participants.
  • Understanding this is key to guide their evolution.

Economic space agency is the power to shape—and benefit from—economic protocols.

2

Economic computation is evolving.

From tablets to blockchains, a short history of innovation in economic computation.

  What it is What it enabled Limitation
Tablet & Tally Stick Computation (~3000 BCE) a shared state. A durable memory for economic agreements, moving logic from the individual mind to a persistent external object. Durable record & basic syntax. It established a formal syntax for accounting (e.g., this many sheep for this much grain), enabling economic trust and coordination to extend beyond immediate personal relationships and memory. Local & static. Computation was manual and confined to the physical object. The logic was local to the specific record-keepers and could not dynamically coordinate across a wide network.
